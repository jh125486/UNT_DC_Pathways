​​Transfer Pathways Program

Dallas College & the UNT Department of Computer Science and Engineering have partnered together to create the Transfer Pathways Program.

This program aims to provide resources found in common UNT CSCE courses to Dallas College students. These resources include course material, mentorships and learning assistance.

Learn More

How it works

​​​Studying at UNT after completing your courses at Dallas College has never been easier. Dallas College is offering computer science & engineering courses with lessons and resources from the University of North Texas.

This allows DC students to seamlessly transfer to UNT and get up-to-speed when arriving at the university.
