​​Future Students

​Dallas College students have the opportunity to learn material from UNT courses, while pursuing their Associate's degree. This is ideal for students looking to continue their education at the university level in a cost-effective manner, and quickly progressing through a bachelors with UNT.

​​Degree Programs

Students of the DC Pathways Program will have the following undergraduate programs to select from, once they've completed their studies at Dallas College.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Calendar of Events

Throughout the Fall and Spring semesters, the UNT Department of Computer Science & Engineering will be hosting various events to help provide information and answer any questions about the DC Pathways Program. These sessions are both available in-person and online, with program coordinators in attendance.
