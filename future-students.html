​​Future Students

​Dallas College students have the opportunity to learn material from UNT courses, while pursuing their Associate's degree. This is ideal for students looking to continue their education at UNT and earn a Bachelor's degree with the Department of Computer Science & Engineering.

​​Degree Programs

Students of the DC Pathways Program will have the following undergraduate programs to select from, once they've completed their studies at Dallas College.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Calendar of Events

Throughout the Fall and Spring semesters, the UNT Department of Computer Science & Engineering will be hosting various events to help provide information and answer any questions about the program. There are two different types of events.

Tabling Events:
This event will include a Pathways coordinator on-site at various Dallas College campuses to discuss the program with visiting students.

Open House Events:
This event will include a presentation, plus Q&A with a Pathways coordinator. No RSVP needed.
